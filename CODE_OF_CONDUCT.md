# Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose
The goal of this Code of Conduct is to ensure a respectful, safe, and inclusive environment for everyone contributing to this repository.

All contributors — maintainers, developers, reviewers, and participants — are expected to follow these rules to maintain a positive and productive collaboration.

---

## 2. Expected Behavior
Contributors are expected to:

- Demonstrate respect and empathy toward others  
- Use welcoming and inclusive language  
- Provide constructive feedback  
- Accept different viewpoints and experiences  
- Follow the project’s workflow and guidelines  
- Help maintain a positive and collaborative environment  

---

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior
The following behaviors are considered unacceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments  
- Personal attacks or insults  
- Sharing private information without consent  
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or work  
- Aggressive, disrespectful, or uncooperative behavior  
- Ignoring project guidelines or maintainers’ instructions  

---

## 4. Reporting Issues
If you observe or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers.

Reports should include:

- A description of the incident  
- The date and context  
- Any relevant screenshots or links  

All reports will be handled confidentially.

---

## 5. Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.  
They may take appropriate action, including:

- Warning the contributor  
- Temporarily or permanently removing access  
- Rejecting or reverting contributions  
- Escalating the issue if necessary  

---
